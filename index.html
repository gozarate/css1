Neo Culture Technology (NCT)

NCT (엔시티), an acronym for ‘Neo Culture Technology’, is a new boy group concept formed by SM Entertainment. They are described as having an unlimited number of members. The group currently has 23 members.

NCT Fandom Name: NCTzen (meaning all the fans are citizens of NCT)

NCT Fan Color: Pearl Neo Champagne

Sub-Units: NCT U, NCT 127, NCT DREAM, WAYV
